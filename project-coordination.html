Why it matters

Managing a renovation or event in Italy from abroad can become expensive very quickly.

Projects in Italy can be difficult to manage from abroad when permits, municipal requirements, building managers, utilities, technical professionals and suppliers all have to be coordinated within an unfamiliar bureaucracy.

Small unresolved uncertainties, scattered updates, unclear estimates and delayed approvals can quickly affect cost, timing and quality. Lianor keeps communication, decisions and follow-up connected on the client's side, ensuring a smooth and seamless experience.

Projects Lianor can coordinate

Property renovations, corporate events and other projects with many moving parts.

Property renovation coordination is the principal service. Corporate event management, conference management and executive meeting support are accepted selectively when a defined local role can reduce risk, save the client time and keep decisions moving.

Property renovation project coordination

From early estimates to handover, Lianor coordinates communication among the client, architects, surveyors, interior designers, renovation contractors, builders, trades, suppliers and delivery teams, keeping meetings, decisions, timelines and site updates organized.

Corporate events, conferences and executive meetings

Selective corporate event management and conference management support covering venues, catering, technical production, speakers, guest logistics, transportation, schedules and on-site follow-up, including executive meetings and high-level private or corporate gatherings.

Property setup, furnishing and handover

For clients preparing a renovated or newly purchased property for use, Lianor can coordinate furniture and appliance deliveries, utility activation, supplier access, final cleaning, punch-list follow-up, key handover and the practical details needed to make the property ready.

What the service can include

One operational view from the first brief to final handover.

Professional boundaries

Clear coordination, with each specialist responsible for their own field.

Lianor coordinates information, people, timelines and follow-through, while technical, architectural, engineering, legal, tax, construction and other regulated services remain the responsibility of appropriately qualified professionals appointed to the project.

Every engagement is scoped in writing so that responsibilities, availability, reporting arrangements and decision authority are clear from the beginning.
